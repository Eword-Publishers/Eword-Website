Our Hybrid Publishing Approach

What is Hybrid Publishing?

At Eword Publishers, we specialize in Hybrid Publishing. This model combines the key benefits of both traditional publishing and self-publishing, offering authors professional support while allowing them to retain creative control and ownership of their work.

Unlike traditional publishing where the publisher assumes the financial risk and controls most aspects, or self-publishing where the author does everything, Hybrid Publishing offers a partnership. Authors invest in professional services provided by Eword Publishers (such as editing, design, and distribution facilitation) to ensure their book meets market standards.

The author is responsible for the production costs, but Eword Publishers assumes a portion of the financial risk during the process by providing expert services aimed at producing a high-quality, marketable book. This model is ideal for authors seeking professional guidance and resources without giving up control over their publishing journey.

Key Benefits of Hybrid with Eword

Flexibility & Control

Retain complete control over your manuscript, design choices, and publishing timeline.

Professional Quality

Access our team of experts for high-quality editing, design, and formatting, ensuring a professional final product.

Time-Saving

We handle the technical and administrative complexities of publishing, allowing you to focus on your writing.

Market Guidance

Benefit from our industry knowledge and receive advice on effective marketing strategies for your book.
