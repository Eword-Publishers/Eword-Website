Getting a Printing Quote

Printing costs are variable and depend on several factors specific to your book. To provide you with an accurate quote for Press Services (Physical Printing), we need the following information:

  • Book Title & Author's Name: For identification.
  • Book Size: Specify the desired size (e.g., A4, A5, 6x9 inches, B5, etc.).
  • Interior Color: Indicate if the interior should be full color or black and white.
  • Quantity: The number of copies you need. (Note: Our Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) for offset printing is 250 copies.)
  • Manuscript Status: Is the manuscript ready for printing (fully formatted, edited, designed), or does it require preliminary services first?

Once we have this information, we can provide a tailored printing quote.
