Our Publishing Services

Preliminary Services

These services prepare your manuscript to give it a professional feel and look, giving it the best possible chance of getting seen and picked up by a potential reader. While highly recommended, they are optional.

Transcription

Convert spoken words, video, or audio recordings into written text. Essential when your manuscript is developed from non-written content.

Pricing: Ksh 30 per Minute of Audio/Video

Content Development

Transforming a raw manuscript into a polished and well-structured book. This process includes reorganizing content, adding explanations, expanding points, and ensuring flow and readability.

Applicable for books up to 180 pages.

Pricing: Ksh 18,500 Per Title

Proofreading & Editing

A comprehensive process for improving the overall quality of your text. We focus on:

  • Grammar, Spelling, Punctuation
  • Syntax and Sentence Structure
  • Vocabulary and Word Choice
  • Style and Tone
  • Continuity and Coherence
  • Clarity and Readability

Applicable for books up to 180 pages.

Pricing: Ksh 14,500 Per Title

(Books 181-300 pages: Ksh 16,500 - refer to FAQ/Pricing for details)

Book Layout (Formatting)

Arranging text, images, and design elements on the pages of a book, ready for printing. This involves determining size, margins, fonts, and overall visual structure.

Applicable for books up to 180 pages.

Pricing: Ksh 8,500 Per Title

(Pricing for larger books depends on size and complexity)

Book Cover Design

Creating a compelling and eye-catching visual concept for your book's cover, spine, and back. Includes the creation of three initial concepts and up to two rounds of edits.

Pricing: Ksh 4,500 Per Title

ISBN Installation

Obtaining and assigning an International Standard Book Number (ISBN) to your book. An ISBN is essential for distribution through booksellers and libraries.

(Authors can also obtain ISBNs directly from KNLS if preferred.)

Pricing: Ksh 1,500 Per Title

Online Publishing (Amazon KDP)

Reach a global audience by making your book available for sale on Amazon. We assist in converting your manuscript and publishing it on Amazon KDP (Kindle Direct Publishing).

Amazon KDP Publishing

Conversion of your manuscript to formats required for Amazon KDP, including EPUB for eBooks and appropriate formats for paperback printing.

Assistance with setting up or accessing your Amazon KDP account.

Includes publishing for both eBook and paperback versions.

Pricing: Ksh 5,500 Per Title

Learn more about the online publishing process and formats on our How It Works page.

Press Services (Physical Printing)

Once your book design is finalized, we provide high-quality offset printing services for physical copies. We handle the entire process from printing to binding and trimming.

Offset Printing

The process of creating physical copies of your book. Our service includes:

  • Printing
  • Folding
  • Collection
  • Binding
  • Lamination
  • Trimming & Packaging

Pricing for printing is quote-based.

Costs vary depending on:

  • Page Count
  • Quantity (Minimum Order Quantity is 250 copies)
  • Book Size
  • Interior Color (Black & White or Full Color)

Please see our Pricing page for details on how quotes are determined.
