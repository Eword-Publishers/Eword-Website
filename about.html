About Us

Our Story

Established in 2015 in Nairobi, Kenya, Eword Publishers is a publishing firm offering an extensive array of printing and publishing services to clients in Kenya and around the world. Our mission is to democratize access to top-tier printing and publishing services, catering to the needs of individuals and organizations alike.

Since our inception, we have successfully published hundreds of thousands of books and numerous other types of publications. By leveraging our extensive industry knowledge, we have formed strategic alliances with other players in the field. These collaborations have uniquely positioned us to cater to the diverse printing and publishing needs of a wide range of businesses.

Over the past seven years, Eword Publishers has also amassed significant experience in institutional and corporate projects, offering a wide variety of digital and offset printing services.
