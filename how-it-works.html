How Our Publishing Process Works

Step-by-Step Guide

We work with your manuscript regardless of its current stage. Our comprehensive process is divided into key stages to bring your book to life.

1. Initial Contact & Project Assessment

You reach out to us with details about your book or project idea. We discuss your goals and the current status of your manuscript.

2. Agreement & Manuscript Submission

Once we understand your needs, we send you the Eword Publishing Agreement for your review and signature. After signing, you provide us with your manuscript.

3. Manuscript Review & Service Recommendation

We review your manuscript to determine if it's ready for printing or if preliminary services (like editing, layout, design) are required. We provide recommendations based on the manuscript's condition and your goals.

4. Quoting & Payment

We provide a detailed quote for the recommended services. Preliminary services and Online Publishing require upfront payment. Printing requires upfront payment, with installment options available before printing commences.

5. Preliminary Services (If Needed)

Our team performs the necessary preliminary services such as transcription, content development, proofreading, editing, book layout, cover design, and ISBN installation based on the agreed-upon quote.

6. Dummy Copy Review (After Preliminary Services)

If preliminary services were performed, we provide you with 2 dummy copies of your book. This allows you to experience the final look and feel and provide any final corrections or suggestions.

7. Final Approval & Preparation for Press/Online

Based on your feedback from the dummy copy review, final adjustments are made. Once you approve the final appearance and design, the book is prepared for either physical printing (Press Services) or online distribution (Amazon KDP Publishing).

8. Press Services (Physical Printing - If Chosen)

If you choose physical copies, the book goes through our offset printing process, including printing, folding, collection, binding, lamination, and trimming.

9. Online Publishing (Amazon KDP - If Chosen)

If you choose online publishing, we convert your manuscript to the required formats and publish it on Amazon KDP, making it available to a global audience.

10. Delivery & Handover

Printed books are delivered to you. For Amazon KDP publishing, the login credentials for the account are handed over to you, giving you full control.

Typical Timelines

Our timelines are estimates and can vary based on the complexity and size of your project.

Preliminary Services (Book Preparation Only): Typically takes 14 working days for a standard book (up to 180 pages). Bigger books may take longer.
Press Services (Printing) Only: Typically takes 14 working days.
Full Process (Preliminary + Press): Typically takes 28 working days (14 days preliminary + 14 days printing) for a standard book.

Timelines can be adjusted accordingly based on specific client needs and project scope.
